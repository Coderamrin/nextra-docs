# Downloading Templates

If you already made payment for a premium template, you will recieve an account activation email from us to your email and this proccess will create an account for you automatically -

**Follow these steps to download your purchased templates:**

### Download Instruction:
1. Activate your account by clicking on the link within the email.
2. [Login to Next.js](https://nextjstemplates.com/login) Templates to download your purchased files.
3. You will be redirected to [purchased downloads](https://nextjstemplates.com/dashboard/downloads) page

---

If you find any difficulties or have any issues, open a [support ticket here](https://nextjstemplates.com/support) we will get back to you ASAP.
